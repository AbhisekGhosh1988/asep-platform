# Executive Summary

This roadmap defines the long-term product evolution of ASEP.

The roadmap is organized into milestones that progressively transform ASEP from a platform foundation into a complete AI-native Software Engineering Platform.

The roadmap focuses on delivering incremental business value while maintaining architectural integrity.

---

# Product Vision

ASEP aims to become the Engineering System of Record for software organizations.

Every milestone contributes toward this vision.

# Guiding Principles

Every roadmap milestone must:

- Deliver measurable customer value.
- Preserve architectural consistency.
- Improve engineering productivity.
- Strengthen enterprise governance.
- Expand engineering knowledge.
- Enhance AI collaboration.

---

# Success Criteria

ASEP will be considered successful when organizations can:

- Engineer software collaboratively.
- Preserve engineering knowledge.
- Generate production-ready software.
- Maintain complete traceability.
- Improve software quality.
- Reduce delivery time.
- Scale engineering across teams.

---

# Roadmap Summary

The ASEP roadmap transforms the platform from an enterprise engineering foundation into a complete AI-native Software Engineering Platform capable of supporting organizations throughout the entire software development lifecycle.
