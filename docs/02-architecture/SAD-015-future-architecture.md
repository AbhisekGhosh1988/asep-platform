# Executive Summary

This document describes the long-term architectural evolution of ASEP.

The platform is intentionally designed to evolve incrementally while preserving business continuity and minimizing architectural disruption.

---

# Evolution Principles

The architecture should:

- Preserve modularity.
- Minimize migration effort.
- Maintain backward compatibility.
- Support incremental adoption.
- Enable enterprise scalability.
